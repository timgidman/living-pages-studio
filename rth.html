QuickStart 4-Question Analyzer

A gentle first step for nervous first-time users. Answer only 4 questions. Then receive a clear recommendation: “Target these 2 areas first.” The findings and scriptures can also be read aloud.

This is a ministry-style reflection tool, not a medical diagnosis. If someone is in immediate danger, call emergency services. In the U.S., call or text 988 for crisis support.

How this works

1

Hear or read

Tap “Hear Question” or simply read it on the screen.

2

Pick one answer

Each question has only 3 simple choices.

3

Get 2 targets

The summary tells you the first 2 areas to work on.

Answer these 4 questions

Q1
What feels heaviest right now?
Q2
What usually sets it off?
Q3
What do you need most today?
Q4
What is causing the most damage right now?
